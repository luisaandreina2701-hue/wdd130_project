Four Worlds, One Country

Ecuador is a small country located in South America, but it offers an incredible diversity of landscapes and cultures. In one country, visitors can explore four completely different regions: the Pacific Coast, the Andes Mountains, the Amazon rainforest, and the Galápagos Islands.
